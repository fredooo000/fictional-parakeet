Method Walkthrough: How to Identify VBV and Non-VBV Cards
 Objective
 Use the Visa Purchase Page to check if a card is VBV (Verified by Visa) or Non-VBV.
 VBV vs Non-VBV- VBV: Requires 3D Secure (OTP/password). Not ideal for unverified platforms.- Non-VBV: No extra authentication. Easier to use.
 Steps:
 1. Go to the VISA PURCHASE PAGE (e.g., Visa Purchase Alerts).
 2. Enter the card number and select the card's issuing country.
 3. Click 'Check eligibility' and observe the result.
 Results Interpretation:- If it proceeds without redirect or shows success -> Non-VBV- If it shows a warning or redirect -> VBV
