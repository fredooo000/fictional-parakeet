# HOW TO DO SAINS REDUCED BARCODES
1. Get your barcode off this sever or the generator 
2. Go into sainsburys
3. Pick up the item/s that u have barcodes for.
4. Go to the self checkout
5.When your there scan your barcodes 1 by 1 and put the item (the barcode is for) on the bagging scale. 1 by 1.
6. Once you have done all that pay quickly (Recomended to pay in cash)
7. Enjoy!!
